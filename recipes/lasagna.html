Lasagna

Home

Description

This classic homemade lasagna layers tender pasta sheets with rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and plenty of melted mozzarella. Slow-simmered tomato sauce brings depth and sweetness, while a touch of Parmesan adds sharp, salty flavor. It’s baked until bubbling and golden on top — the perfect comfort food for family dinners or Sunday gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara or tomato sauce
  • 2 cups ricot cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cut grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Szlt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basic or parsley, for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the lasagna noodles until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and cook until soft, then stir in garlic.
  4. Add ground beef or sausage, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  5. Stir in the marinara sauce and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, egg, half the mozzarella, and half the Parmesan.
  7. Spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Add a layer of noodles, then spread ricotta mixture and sauce on top. Repeat for 3 layers.
  8. Top the final layer with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 20 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  10. Let the lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing. Garnish with basil or parsley if desired.
